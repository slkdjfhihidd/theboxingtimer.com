Why this app exists

Made by a boxer, for boxers

Boxing Timer was built by someone who got tired of timers that quit the moment the screen locked, silenced the bell behind a playlist, or buried round setup under menus mid-session. Every decision in this app answers one question: does it survive a real training session in a real gym?

And rounds aren't only for boxing. The same work-and-rest rhythm runs Muay Thai, MMA, kickboxing, HIIT circuits, and conditioning — so the app ships with a dedicated HIIT tab and fully custom intervals. If your training has a clock, this timer runs it.

No data collected. None.

Most free apps pay for themselves with your data. This one doesn't — it's kept free by a single sponsor card instead, so there is nothing about you to collect.

  • No account, ever. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to leak.
  • No analytics, no ad tracking. Your presets and history live on your phone and nowhere else.
  • One anonymous request. The app's only network call fetches the public sponsor card — identical for every user, nothing sent about you.
