One brand. Every workout. No ad network.

Boxing Timer is free and stays free because of a single sponsor slot inside the app. Not programmatic ads — one brand at a time, presented as a clean card on the home screen that users see every time they set up a session.

Who you reach

People who train: boxers, Muay Thai and MMA fighters, kickboxers, HIIT athletes, and the coaches and gyms that run their rounds. They open the app on purpose, several times a week, in the exact moment they're thinking about training gear, recovery, and coaching.

What a sponsorship includes

  • The only branded slot in the app — your name, logo, tagline, and link on the home screen card
  • An optional promo code displayed on the card, so you can measure redemptions directly in your own store
  • A date-bound campaign — your sponsorship starts and ends exactly when agreed, updated remotely without waiting on app updates
  • Exclusivity — one sponsor at a time, never a rotation you share

What we don't do

No tracking, no user profiling, no retargeting — the app collects no user data, and that's a promise we keep for sponsors too. Your card is never placed where it would interrupt training: not on the gym TV display, not in widgets, never mid-round.
